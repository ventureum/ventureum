Whitepaper

HOW WE PROTECT INVESTORS

Milestone-driven

Project founders can’t run off with your investments as the funds are locked in predefined milestones. Investors vote to decide whether the objectives of a milestone have been fulfilled and thus whether the funds are released to project founders.

Community-governed

Implementing the spirit of Decentralized Autonomous Organization, the progress of a project is supervised and governed by the community of investors. The ownership of project tokens entitles you voting rights. You, together with other investors, decide the fate of the project you invested.

Ultimate Investment Protection

Investors get refund (in ETH) if they are not satisfied. Furthermore, Ventureum builds on open source smart contracts running on the Ethereum blockchain, with total transparency and openness at all stages.

FAQ

What is Ventureum?

Ventureum is a community-governed milestone-driven crowdfunding platform for blockchain projects. It builds on the Ethereum blockchain. It works as a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) in which investors who have a stake in blockchain projects can vote to monitor and influence the progress of a project, and get a (partial) refund if the progress fails to meet their expectations.

What does the word ‘Ventureum’ stand for?

The word ‘Ventureum’ is a portmanteau of ‘venture’ and ‘eum’ (place) which means a place or a platform for (blockchain) venture projects.

How does Ventureum distinguish itself from other blockchain crowdfunding platforms?

To our best knowledge, Ventureum is the first ever proposed blockchain crowdfunding platform in which investors have influence over the progress of a project, and particularly the release of funds (ETH) raised in a crowdsale to project founders. It implements a milestone-driven funds management mechanism in which investors can vote to decide the objectives of a milestone have been met. In case of a milestone failure, investors are eligible for a refund (ETH).

What is the name of the token?

The token is named VTH, demonstrating its connection with the Ethereum ecosystem.

What is the use of VTH?

VTH gives its holders full access to the Ventureum platform's services, features, and functionalities, particularly coverage for a refund in the Milestone-driven Funds Management module.

Can I mine VTH?

No. The Ventureum platform resides on the Ethereum blockchain. The VTH tokens are created during contribution periods. No mining is required to create VTH tokens.
